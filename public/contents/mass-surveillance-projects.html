Mass Surveillance Projects

They are summarized here because the information of these projects are big, for more information please click their name.

NSA

  • XKeyscore - Collects Internet activity, emails, chats, browsing histories & more.
  • FASCIA - Collects location data of people around the world.
  • Optic Nerve - Collects private webcam still images from users while they are using a Yahoo! webcam application
  • Boundless Informant - Boundless Informant is a big data analysis and data visualization tool used by the United States National Security Agency.
  • Dishfire - Collects text messages on a daily basis from around the world.
  • Mystic - Collects telephone calls, including metadata and a full record call’s contents.
  • [Muscular](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Muscular_(surveillance_program) - Wiretaps of Google and Yahoo unencrypted internal networks.
  • MainWay - NSA database containing metadata of hundreds of billions of telephone calls.
  • BullRun - Eavesdrop on encrypted communications by nfluencing and weakening encryption standards, by obtaining master encryption keys, and by gaining access to data before or after it is encrypted either by agreement, by force of law, or by computer network exploitation(hacking).
  • FAMS Quiet Skies - Surveillance travelers.
