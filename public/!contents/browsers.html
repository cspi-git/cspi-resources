Browsers
...

Please check https://spyware.neocities.org/ for spyware audit information of some browsers.

  • Brave - Is fast, secure, and privacy-focused by default with a built-in ad blocker. BEST
  • Librewolf - A fork of firefox, focused on privacy, security, and freedom.
  • Tor - Defend yourself against tracking and surveillance. Circumvent censorship. BEST
  • Epic - Privacy focused web browser.
  • Bromite - Bromite is a Chromium fork with ad blocking and enhanced privacy; take back your browser. BEST
  • Firefox - Privacy-first web browser with lots of addons to further increase privacy. BEST
  • Pale Moon - Pale Moon is an Open Source, Goanna-based web browser available for Microsoft Windows and Linux (with contributed other operating system support), focusing on efficiency and customization. Make sure to get the most out of your browser!
  • Chromium - Chromium is a free and open-source web browser project, principally developed and maintained by Google.
  • Waterfox - Waterfox is an open-source web browser for x64, ARM64, and PPC64LE systems. It is intended to be ethical and maintain support for legacy extensions dropped by Firefox, from which it is forked.
  • Iridium - An open source browser that highly focuses in speed, privacy & ease of use.
  • SnowHaze - SnowHaze offers a browser with your privacy and security in mind. It blocks invasive ads and trackers, protects you from browser fingerprinting, is lightning fast, and much more.
  • Vanadium - Privacy and security enhanced releases of Chromium for GrapheneOS.
