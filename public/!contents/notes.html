  • Laverna - An open source & encrypted note-taking app.
  • Obsidian - Obsidian is not an old note-taking app like Evernote but like Roam Research. This app will be your second brain with your notes. Obsidian is one of the best Markdown note-taking app in the world. BEST
  • DeepNotes - An open source end-to-end encrypted infinite canvas with deep bidirectional page navigation and live collaboration. BEST
  • Notesnook - Notesnook is a security-focused note-taking app. Security is the main sailing point of this note-taking app.
  • Bangle - Bangle.io is a powerful yet simple note taking web app that works on top of your locally saved Markdown files.
  • SafeRoom - Saferoom is a note-taking app that allows users to create encrypted notes, including PDF, video, audio and images. The app protects data by encrypting the data before syncing with other devices. Saferoom ensures that the data always stays encrypted and secured against leaks.
  • Bear - Flexible writing app for crafting notes and prose.
  • EteSync - Secure, end-to-end encrypted, and privacy respecting sync for your contacts, calendars, tasks and notes.
  • Joplin - Open source note taking app that supports end to end encrypted sync wth cloud providers.
  • Standard Notes - Is a safe place for your notes, thoughts, and life's work. BEST
  • TinyList - Create and share notes and checklists, without sacrificing your privacy.
  • HedgeDoc - Formerly CodiMD (community). An awesome platform to write and share markdown.
  • Notally - A beautiful notes app.
  • Quillnote - Take beautiful markdown notes and stay organized with task lists.
  • Logseq - A privacy-first alternative to WorkFlowy. BEST
  • Nextcloud Notes - The Notes app is a distraction free notes taking app for Nextcloud.
    • Nextcloud Notes app - An android client for Nextcloud Notes.
  • Vikunja - The open-source to-do app to organize your life.
  • AppFlowy - Open Source Notion Alternative. You are in charge of your data and customizations.
