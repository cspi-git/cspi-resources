Using misinformation to make doxers doubt what they have.md
...

This technique is really great and it's a lot better if you execute it earlier. "Early planned attacks are good or making traps parallels ahead of your enemy is good.", if you want to have fun while protecting your privacy, you can use misinformation mostly if you are in some tight positions in protecting your privacy. Misleading your enemy, isn't that really hard, it's all about knowing what they might or will check.

Here is a quick example:
So you are a hacker from a hackers forum but for some reason people hate you because of the way you talk, and somehow you noticed that some people may be trying to dox you, but you already know you have secured your privacy on that subject but want to make it interesting.

Probably since you are on a hackers forum, you already know how to think like a hacker and know what the boxers will do first in finding you. If not, it's very obvious the first thing or instinct that they will have to search for is your username.

Second, they will scrape and look deeply in your profile and stuff like content and possible emails but since you have secured your or this subject, you don't worry about it, because they will find nothing but dead-ends. Now to make it interesting, you can just post some fake information about you in pastes websites that they might visit or other stuff. Well, that is basically it.

How to fully messsage anonymously

This technique will teach you how to fully message anonymously but this guide is not your standard guide; it's a bit hard and it requires an 8 GB USB and the reason it's hard is because it requires patience and discipline.

So let's get started:

  1. Flash Tails in the USB and make sure it's not persistent.
  2. Install privacy-friendly messaging software like Session, Wickr, SimpleX and Wickr.
  3. Your done!

Now let's say you are done with your Sus business or work all you have to do is shutdown Tails OS then hide the USB somewhere or if you want to go extreme, just completely format it and if you want to go Sus business again or work flash Tails OS again on it.

Let's say you want to it to be persistent:

  1. Flash any Linux in the USB and make sure it's persistent, but for this part you will need a 10 GB USB or 15 GB USB.
  2. Install privacy-friendly messaging software like Session, Wickr, SimpleX and Wickr.
  3. Install Tor and make it run on every startup. Help here.

Same as above, if you are on a Sus business or something just shutdown the OS, then hide the USB.
